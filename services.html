Our Pathology Services

We offer a wide range of reliable and quick diagnostic services at your doorstep. Accuracy, convenience, and timely reports are our top priorities.

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Evaluates your overall health and detects a wide range of disorders like anemia and infection.

Blood Sugar Test (Fasting & PP)

Measures blood glucose levels for diagnosing and monitoring diabetes.

Lipid Profile

Assesses cholesterol levels to evaluate heart health and risk factors.

Liver Function Test (LFT)

Checks liver health by measuring enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin levels.

Kidney Function Test (KFT)

Analyzes blood and urine to assess kidney performance and detect issues.

Thyroid Function Test (T3, T4, TSH)

Monitors thyroid gland activity and diagnoses related disorders.

Vitamin D Test

Checks vitamin D levels important for bone health and immune support.

Vitamin B12 Test

Detects B12 deficiencies that can lead to fatigue, nerve problems, and anemia.

HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)

Reflects average blood sugar levels over the past 3 months for diabetes control.

Urine Routine & Microscopy

Identifies infections, kidney problems, and metabolic conditions.

Stool Test

Used to detect digestive issues, parasites, and blood in the stool.

Electrolyte Panel

Monitors levels of sodium, potassium, and other electrolytes for proper body function.

Prostate-Specific Antigen (PSA) Test

Early detection tool for prostate health issues, including cancer.

C-Reactive Protein (CRP)

Measures inflammation in the body to diagnose infections or chronic diseases.

COVID-19 RT-PCR Test

Highly accurate test for active COVID-19 infection with doorstep sample collection.

Dengue NS1 Antigen Test

Detects early-stage dengue fever to ensure timely treatment.

Malaria Antigen Test

Quick and effective test for diagnosing malaria infections.

Allergy Panel

Identifies sensitivities to dust, pollen, food, and other common allergens.

Hormone Tests (LH, FSH, Prolactin, etc.)

Evaluates reproductive and hormonal balance for both men and women.

Pathology Packages (Full Body Checkups)

Comprehensive packages tailored for men, women, seniors, and corporate professionals to promote preventive care.
